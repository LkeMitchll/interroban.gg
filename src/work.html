Process

My aim is to help clients to build great solutions for the people that need them. This means combining user-centred design with business needs to discover the real problem, define the solution, iterate, test, and refine the solution. Over the years I have formed my own framework for project success 1. The process is not linear, starting out uncertain and messy, becoming more refined and focused as time goes on.

Nº1 I have been strongly influenced by the 'Double Diamond' framework, the 'Lean' methodology, and many other design thinking processes. ↩
